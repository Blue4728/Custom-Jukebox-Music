BeatCrafter

What does this tool do? It generates a .mcpack resource pack that replaces Minecraft music discs with your own songs.

Note: Minecraft only supports .ogg audio files. If your songs are .mp3, convert them to .ogg here:

  • FreeConvert (Web, slow)
  • Audio Converter (App, fast)
